# Mock Pink Champagne

Origin: Unknown, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1  6 oz can frozen orange juice & 1 of grapefruit
- 1 big bottle ginger ale
- 1/3 cup grenadine
- 1/2 big bottle club soda

## Directions

Boil sugar & water - mix juices & etc. Add syrup to taste
