# Apple Pie

Origin: Unknown, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 lbs apples - pared (8 c.) evenly sliced
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 2 Tb flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 Tb margarine

## Directions

Place apples in large bowl - mix sugars & spices - & mix with apples - let stand till juice forms. Pour into 9 in. pastry. Bake at 425Ëš for 40 minutes.

(for crispy top, brush pastry with a little water or milk & sprinkle with sugar)
