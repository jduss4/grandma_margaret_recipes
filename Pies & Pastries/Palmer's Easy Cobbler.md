# Easy Cobbler

Origin: E. Palmer, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients and Directions

Mix together:

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 2 tsp baking powder

Melt 1 stick butter - pour in above batter

1 qt canned fruit - sprinkle with sugar & cinnamon
