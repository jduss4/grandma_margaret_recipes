# Easy Country Supper

Origin: Unknown, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 4 thinly sliced potatoes
- 3 sliced carrots
- 1/2 cup sliced celery
- 3 Tb vegetable oil
- (salt and pepper)

## Directions

Fry until p & c are tender

Place Hillshire Farm smoked sausage on top. Cover & heat 12 minutes.
