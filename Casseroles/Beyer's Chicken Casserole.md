# Chicken Casserole

Origin: M. Beyer, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cubed chicken
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 1 can mushroom soup
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 3/4 cup celery
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 2 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions
