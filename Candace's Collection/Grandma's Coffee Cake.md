# Grandma's Coffee Cake

Origins: Grandma Lily, Gma Margaret's grandma

## Ingredients

- 1/4 c sugar (scant)
- 1/2 c Crisco
- 2 beaten eggs
- 3/4 c milk
- 1/2 t vanilla
- 1/2 c nuts
- 3/4 c raisins
- 1/4 t salt
- 3 c flour
- 3 t baking powder

## Directions

Mix as for regular cake

Makes stiff dough - sprinkle with cinnamon & sugar over top after placing in greased loaf pan.

Bake at 350Ëš 35 mins. or longer. Test center with toothpick - if sticky bake until pick is not sticky.

Split into 2 pans - bake ~ 30 min
