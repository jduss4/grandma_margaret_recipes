# Carrot Cake

Origin: Says "M. Engel", grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 1/2 tsp soda
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup Crisco oil
- 2 cup shredded carrots
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- small can crushed pineapple ( do not drain)

## Directions

Mix as for regular cake

Bake one hour at 350Ëš

## Icing

- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1/2 stick oleo or butter
- 1 tsp vanilla

Cream: add 2 cups powdered sugar
