# Oatmeal Cake

Origins: Unknown, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients and Directions

Stir together & let stand

- 1 c quick Quaker oats
- 1 1/4 boiling water

Cream

- 1 stick oleo
- 1 c white sugar
- 1 c brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 t vanilla

Add to oatmeal

- 1 1/4 c flour
- 1 t cinnamon
- 1 t soda

Add this to creamed sugars etc

Bake 350Ëš in flat greased pan 30 m

## Frosting

- 6 Tb melted marg
- 3/4 c brown sugar
- 1 cup coconut
- 1/2 chop nuts
- 2 t vanilla
- 1/4 milk

Spread on top & bake another 5 or 6 min
