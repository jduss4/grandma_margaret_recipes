# Almond Roca Cookies

Origin: Grandma (TODO check with mom if actually a different generation's recipe)

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Crisco
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg yoke [sic]
- 1 10 oz milk chocolate bar

## Directions

Cream Crisco, sugar, vanilla, and slightly beaten egg yolk. Blend in flour. Spread evenly on cookie sheet (ungreased) on with sides.

Bake at 350Ëš for 15 - 20 mins. Break up chocolate bar (I sliver with sharp knife) & drop on top of hot cookie dough. Spread evenly when melted. Sprinkle 1 c. firmly chopped nuts on top.
