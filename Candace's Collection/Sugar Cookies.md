# Sugar Cookies

Origin: Mom (Candace) thinks this might come from the English relatives part of the family tree

## Ingredients

- 1 c butter
- 1 c sugar
- 1 egg
- 4 c flour - keep out 1/2 c for rolling
- 1/2 c milk
- 1/4 t soda
- 1 Tb hot water
- 1 t vanilla
