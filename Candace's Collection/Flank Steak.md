# Flank Steak

Origins:  Margaret Beyer, a friend of Margaret's from Santa Ana church

## Ingredients and Directions

[ grouped ]

- 1 t salt
- 1/4 t pepper
- 1 t garlic salt

Rub into both sides of flank steak - press hard. Marinate 3 hours in :

- 1 Tb lemon juice
- 1 Tb vinegar
- 1 Tb oil
- 1 t oregano

Broil and slice diagonally
