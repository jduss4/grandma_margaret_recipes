# Apple Crisp

Origin: Unknown, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients and Directions

- 5 apples, peeled, cored & sliced
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Cream together:

- 1/2 cup oleo
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup flour

Put first 4 ingredients in pan. Layer with last 3. Bake in 350Ëš oven for 1 hour.
