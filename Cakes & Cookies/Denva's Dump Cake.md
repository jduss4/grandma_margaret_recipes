# Dump Cake

Origin: Denva Kinder, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 1  3 1/2 oz can crushed pineapple
- 1 can cherry pie filling
- 1/2 cup nuts (optional)
- 1 yellow cake mix (2 layer size)
- 1 1/2 sticks margerine
