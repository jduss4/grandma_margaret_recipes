# Chop Suey Cake

Origin: A. Forbush, grandma's handwriting

## Cake

Mix together:

- 2 cup flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup nuts
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tsp soda
- 1 lg can crushed pineapple

Mix & pour into 9x13 pan*. Bake 35 to 40 mins. at 350Ëš (* grease & lightly flour)

## Frosting

- 1 lg cream cheese
- 1 stick oleo
- 1/2 [box?] & powdered sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 2 Tb milk

Mix & pour over warm cake.
