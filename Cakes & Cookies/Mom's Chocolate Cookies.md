# Mom's Chocolate Cookies

Origin: Grandma's mother, Agnes Lilian (Hurwood) Deyarmond

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter creamed with
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 3 Tb cocoa

## Directions

Drop from spoon in pan & bake - put 2 together & frost

About 300˚ - 350˚
