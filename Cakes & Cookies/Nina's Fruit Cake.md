# Fruit Cake

Origin: Nina Clark, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients and Directions

Sift together

- 3/4 cup flour
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt

Place in bowl:

- 3 cup (1 lb brazils)
- 1 lb dates
- 1 cup well-drained red & green marashino [sic] cherries

Sift flour mixture into above - mix with hands till fruit & nuts are well-coated.

3 eggs - beat till foamy

1 tsp vanilla - add to 1st mixture.

Bake at 300Ëš 1 hour & 45 mins. in loaf pan greased & lined with wax paper. Cool 15 mins. Remove wax paper. Wrap in foil - refrigerate.
