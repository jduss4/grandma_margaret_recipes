# Apple Nut Cake

Origin: Unknown, typed card

## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup oil Kraft or Crisco
- 2 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 2 cups apples, sliced

## Directions

Beat in order all together, last add nuts and apples. Fold together.

Bake 350 (45 - 55 minutes)
