# Scotch Shortbread

Origin: Unknown, Grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 1/4 cup sifted flour

## Directions

Cream butter, add sugar & continue creaming. Then work in each cake flour.

Kneed [ tweer ? ] - Prick with fork & bake in moderate oven (350ËšF) 45 minutes.
