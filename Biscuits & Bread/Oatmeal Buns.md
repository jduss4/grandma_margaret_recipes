# Oatmeal Buns

Origin: Unknown, grandma's handwriting

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 cup dates
- 2 cups rolled oats
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp soda
- 2 cups flour
- 1 1/2 to 2 tsp lemon extract

## Directions

Cream Crisco - sugar, add beaten eggs & milk. Add dates then flour, b.p., soda & salt. Add dates & lemon. Drop on greased baking sheet. Bake about 12 mins at 350ËšF.
