# Kedgeree

Origin: Unknown, not sure my grandma's handwriting but similar?

## Ingredients

- 2 cups salmon or haddock
- 2 onions chopped
- butter
- 1 Tb curry powder
- 1 green pepper chopped
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- 2 cups cooked rice

## Directions

Saut√© onions & pepper in butter until onion is golden. Add curry powder, salt, pepper, chopped egg whites, fish, & rice with 2 Tb butter. Mix well & beat thru. Serve piled high with egg yolks served on top.
