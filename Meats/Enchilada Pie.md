# Enchilada Pie

Origin: Says "From the kitchen of M. Engel", my grandma, but I'm dubious she came up with this one :)

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground round
- 1 onion chopped
- 1 Tb salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1 Tb chili powder
- 1  8oz tomato sauce
- 6 tortillas (flour) spread with butter
- 1 can chopped ripe olives
- 1 1/2 cup grated cheese
- 1 cup water

## Directions

Brown beef & onion. Add seasonings & tomato sauce. In covered casserole alternate layers of buttered tortillas, meat sauce, olives & cheese. Add water. Cover & bake in hot oven (400Ëš) for 20 min.
